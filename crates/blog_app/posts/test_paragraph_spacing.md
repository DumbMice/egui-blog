### Very Long Paragraph Example

This paragraph is intentionally very long to test how the spacing system handles content that spans many lines. In typography, the relationship between line spacing (leading) and paragraph spacing is crucial for readability. Line spacing within a paragraph should be tight enough to keep lines visually connected, while paragraph spacing should be significantly larger to create clear separation between distinct ideas. When these two spacing values are too similar, readers can struggle to identify where one thought ends and another begins. This is particularly important in digital typography where screen reading adds additional cognitive load. By ensuring paragraph spacing is at least 1.5 times the line spacing, we create a visual hierarchy that guides the reader through the content naturally.
