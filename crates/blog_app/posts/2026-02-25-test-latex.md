## Testing LaTeX Math Rendering

### Inline Math
Here's some inline math: $E = mc^2$ and $\frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{4} = \frac{3}{4}$.

### Display Math
Display math should be centered:

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

### More Complex Examples
The quadratic formula:

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

Matrix example:

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9
\end{bmatrix}
$$

### Mixed Content
You can have text with $inline\ math$ and then more text.
